# Enterprise Partners

Singularity Layer Enterprise lets platforms integrate agentic commerce directly into their ecosystem. Your agents deploy x402 endpoints, recharge credits, and transact — all under your brand, with revenue shared automatically.

## What You Get

- **White-label landing page** at `studio.x402layer.cc/enterprise/{your-slug}` — branded with your logo, description, and custom pricing
- **Partner dashboard** to track revenue, manage webhooks, generate API keys, and configure pricing
- **Volume-based revenue sharing** — earn up to 85% of every deployment fee and credit recharge, scaling with your monthly volume
- **Enterprise REST API** — authenticate with `X-Enterprise-Key` to list endpoints, query revenue, and manage your program programmatically
- **MCP integration** — your agents use the Singularity MCP server with an `enterprise_partner_slug` parameter to route actions through your program
- **Webhook notifications** — get notified on deployments, recharges, and payments with HMAC-signed payloads

## How It Works

1. **Onboarding** — We create your enterprise partner account with your branding, pricing preferences, and payout wallet
2. **Configure** — Log into your dashboard, set pricing, generate API keys, configure webhooks
3. **Agent integration** — Your agents call the Singularity MCP server or REST API with your enterprise key
4. **Endpoints deploy** — Agents create x402 endpoints through your program, paying your configured deployment fee
5. **Revenue flows** — Every payment is split and tracked in a transparent ledger visible from your dashboard
6. **Tier upgrades** — As your 30-day volume grows, your revenue share automatically increases (70% → 80% → 85%)
