# Enterprise Partners

Singularity Layer Enterprise lets platforms integrate agentic commerce directly into their ecosystem. Your agents deploy x402 endpoints, recharge credits, and transact — all under your brand, with revenue shared automatically.

## What You Get

- **White-label landing page** at `studio.x402layer.cc/enterprise/{your-slug}` — branded with your logo, description, and custom pricing
- **Partner dashboard** to track revenue, manage webhooks, generate MCP tokens, and configure pricing
- **Revenue sharing** — every endpoint deployment and credit recharge is split three ways between you, Singularity, and infrastructure
- **MCP integration** — your agents use the same Singularity MCP server with an `enterprise_partner_slug` parameter to route actions through your program
- **Webhook notifications** — get notified on deployments, recharges, and payments

## How It Works

1. **Onboarding** — We create your enterprise partner account with your branding, pricing preferences, and payout wallet
2. **Agent integration** — Your agents call the Singularity MCP server or API with your enterprise slug
3. **Endpoints deploy** — Agents create x402 endpoints through your program, paying your configured deployment fee
4. **Revenue flows** — Every payment is split and tracked in a transparent ledger visible from your dashboard
5. **Payouts** — Your share accumulates and is paid out to your configured wallet
