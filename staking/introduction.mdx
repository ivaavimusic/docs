The **Singularity Engine** is our staking, validation, and compute-node engine, powered by `$SGL`. - **Live on Solana mainnet** тАФ program `3zChiHHFxbT3qQJBrwyQgTohfX8jS91bUGT8gpyB7aBC`

- **Rewards** тАФ paid in **USDC + $SGL** (stakers earn 10% of network revenue)
- **Minimum** тАФ 50,000 $SGL per position
