# $SGL Staking

Staking `$SGL` puts skin-in-the-game behind the Singularity network. Stakers earn a share of network revenue, and staking is what lets you **run a compute node**, **validate the marketplace**, or simply **earn yield**.

- **Live on Solana mainnet** ‚Äî program `3zChiHHFxbT3qQJBrwyQgTohfX8jS91bUGT8gpyB7aBC`
- **Token** ‚Äî `$SGL` mint `5c4HyD2rSShqnTsf5z3SaoD2H3GE452u2CUuYjviBAGS` (9 decimals)
- **Rewards** ‚Äî paid in **USDC + $SGL** (stakers earn 10% of network revenue)
- **Minimum** ‚Äî 50,000 $SGL per position

## Roles (stake types)

| Role | Use | Cooldown |
|------|-----|----------|
| `compute` | Run a compute node | 1 day |
| `validator` | Validate marketplace / ERC-8004 | 2 days |
| `yield` | Earn rewards only | 12 hours |

Each wallet has one position per role. Unstaking starts a cooldown; after it elapses you withdraw.

## Agentic access: prepare ‚Üí sign ‚Üí submit

Staking is an on-chain Anchor program, so we never custody your stake. Mutating actions return an **unsigned** transaction you sign locally with your keypair ‚Äî the signature *is* the proof of ownership (the program rejects any signer that isn't the staker). Reads are public on-chain data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare">POST a prepare endpoint with your wallet ‚Üí get an unsigned base64 transaction.</Step>
  <Step title="Sign">Sign it with your wallet keypair.</Step>
  <Step title="Submit">POST it to `/api/agent/submit` (or broadcast via your own RPC).</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>This is **not** x402. x402 moves a token to a payee; staking locks your *own* tokens in a contract only you control, which requires your signature.</Note>

```bash
# Read (no auth)
curl https://staking.x402layer.cc/api/agent/analytics
curl "https://staking.x402layer.cc/api/agent/positions?wallet=<YOUR_WALLET>"

# Prepare a stake (returns an unsigned tx to sign + submit)
curl -X POST https://staking.x402layer.cc/api/agent/stake \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"wallet":"<YOUR_WALLET>","role":"compute","amount":50000}'
```

See the **[API Reference ‚Üí Staking](/api-reference/staking-stake)** for every endpoint, request/response schema, and a try-it console.

## How rewards work

Rewards are **pro-rata by stake weight and accrue continuously** ‚Äî there is **no fixed APR and no emission schedule**. Each time network revenue is distributed, every staker is credited in proportion to their share of the total staked *at that moment*.

### Where rewards come from

Revenue from compute jobs is split four ways:

| Share | Goes to |
|-------|---------|
| **80%** | Node operator |
| **5%** | Platform |
| **10%** | **Stakers** ‚Äî paid as 5% USDC + 5% $SGL (the $SGL leg is bought on-market) |
| **5%** | Burned ($SGL buy-and-burn) |

USDC and $SGL rewards accrue on **two independent streams** and never mix ‚Äî USDC is paid in USDC, $SGL in $SGL.

### Your share

For any distribution:

```
your reward = distribution_amount √ó (your_stake / total_staked)
```

Example ‚Äî **1,000 USDC** distributed while **2,000,000 $SGL** is staked:

- each staked $SGL earns `1000 / 2,000,000 = 0.0005 USDC`
- a **100,000 $SGL** position (5% of the pool) earns **50 USDC**
- a **500 $SGL** position earns **0.25 USDC**

### Tier-agnostic

Rewards go to **all active stakers weighted purely by amount, regardless of role**. A Yield position and a Validator position of the same size earn exactly the same ‚Äî tiers differ only in **minimum stake** and **cooldown**, never in reward share.

### Eligibility & fairness

- **Only `active` positions earn.** The moment you start unstaking, your position stops accruing new rewards ‚Äî but everything already accrued is locked in and stays claimable through the cooldown.
- **No retroactive rewards, no dilution.** You only earn from distributions made *after* you stake. Stake right after a distribution and you get nothing from it; unstake before one and you miss it. A newcomer can never claw back rewards already owed to existing stakers.
- **Claim any time.** Rewards sit in your position until you claim ‚Äî claiming sends both USDC and $SGL straight to your wallet in one transaction, and works even if the program is paused.

<Note>Rewards are a **pull**: they never auto-transfer. Claim from the [dashboard](https://staking.x402layer.cc) or the agentic API whenever you like.</Note>
