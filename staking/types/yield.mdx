A **Yield Enjoyer** position is the simplest way to participate: stake `$SGL`, earn your share of network revenue, and claim whenever you like. No hardware, no attestations, no operational duties.

## At a glance

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Minimum stake | 50,000 $SGL |
| Cooldown | 12 hours (shortest) |
| Slashable | **Never** — Yield positions are exempt by design |
| Earns staking rewards | Yes (same pro-rata share as every tier) |

## Why pick Yield

- **Passive.** Stake and you're done. Rewards accrue continuously; you just claim.
- **Shortest cooldown.** 12 hours to unwind — the quickest exit of any tier.
- **Zero slashing exposure.** The program has a hard guard: a Yield position **cannot be slashed**, not even by the platform. Your principal can only ever leave the vault back to *you*.

## Earnings

You earn the standard **10% staker share** of network revenue (USDC + $SGL), pro-rata to your stake — the *same* per-token rate a Compute or Validator position of equal size receives. Tier never changes your reward rate. See [How Rewards Work](/staking/rewards/overview).

<Note>Yield Enjoyer is the recommended starting point if you simply want exposure to staking rewards. You can open a Compute or Validator position later if you want to operate or validate.</Note>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stake">Open a Yield position with at least 50,000 $SGL via the [dashboard](https://staking.x402layer.cc) or [agentic API](/staking/agentic/overview).</Step>
  <Step title="Earn">Rewards accrue automatically as the network earns revenue.</Step>
  <Step title="Claim">[Claim](/staking/rewards/claiming) your USDC + $SGL anytime — even during a pause.</Step>
</Steps>
